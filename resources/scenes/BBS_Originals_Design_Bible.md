# BBS Originals - Production Design Bible
## Visual Language Guide for All Scenes

---

## **FRAMING SEGMENT: SOLARIA (The Narrator's Hub)**

### **Atmosphere & Concept**
Celestial command center bridging ancient mythology with futuristic technology. Think "Mount Olympus meets Sci-Fi control room" - a place where cosmic stories are managed and monitored.

### **Visual Language**
- **Primary Colors:** Midnight Blue (#191970), Gold (#FFD700), Silver (#C0C0C0)
- **Accent Colors:** Radiant White for Solarian glow effects
- **Lighting:** Crisp, cool white key lighting on Apollo/Stellar to emphasize metallic costume textures
- **Background:** Star curtain with fiber optic points or LED fairy lights for depth
- **Floor:** High-gloss black or white marley for reflective starlight effects

### **Technical Specifications**
- **The Glitch Effect:** When portal opens, transition lighting to aggressive strobe between blue and purple
- **Props:** Two sleek white pedestals for narrators - minimal, clean aesthetic
- **Costume Notes:** Metallic fabrics that catch and reflect the cool lighting

## **TALE 1: ZORO'S STRIPES (The Savannah)**

### **Overall Visual Philosophy**
Warm, expansive landscapes with high contrast between sun and shadow. Rhythm and movement through natural patterns.

---

### **Scene 1: Savannah's Pride (The Storm)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Color Palette:** Burnt Orange (#CC5500), Sunset Yellow (#FFD300), Deep Brown
- **Lighting Approach:** Golden hour wash with critical backlighting for animal silhouettes
- **Storm Effect:** Gobo rotator with cloud breakup pattern, color shift from warm orange to desaturated grey-green (#556B2F)
- **Set Pieces:** Pride Rock tiered platforms in sandstone textures
- **Atmospheric Elements:** Dust particles in lighting beams, heat shimmer effects

**Technical Notes:**
- Use warm amber gels (Rosco #02, #05) for golden hour
- Cloud gobo: Rosco 77556 or similar breakup pattern
- Strobe units for lightning effects during tornado sequence

---

### **Scene 2: The Seeking of Truth (Jungle)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Color Palette:** Emerald Green (#50C878), Deep Indigo shadows
- **Lighting:** Side-lighting with leaf breakup gobos for dappled effect
- **Texture Elements:** Hanging vine ropes, fabric strips dyed in jungle tones
- **Key Prop:** Raffy's Tree with glowing fruits (LED spheres in translucent plastic)
- **Shadow Play:** Complex leaf patterns on floor and actors

**Technical Notes:**
- Leaf gobos: Rosco 77535 or custom jungle patterns
- Side lighting units at 45-degree angles
- LED puck lights inside fruit props with battery packs

---

### **Scene 3: Tigris' Camp (Martial Arts Dojo)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Color Palette:** Bamboo Tan (#F5DEB3), Red Accents (#FF0000), Black Calligraphy
- **Lighting:** Sharp, angular beams - transition from soft nature to defined dojo lighting
- **Set Elements:** Bamboo fencing panels on rolling frames
- **Decorative Elements:** Vertical banners with paw print motifs
- **Floor Treatment:** Tatami mat textures or bamboo patterned marley

**Technical Notes:**
- Remove nature gobos, use clean spotlights with sharp edges
- Red accent lighting for dramatic moments
- Bamboo panels: lightweight, rollable for quick scene changes

---

### **Scene 4: Evans District (Neon Jungle Celebration)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Color Palette:** Neon Pink (#FF6EC7), Electric Blue (#7DF9FF), Lime Green
- **Lighting:** UV blacklight dominance with disco ball effects
- **Texture Shift:** From natural to synthetic - glossy, reflective surfaces
- **Hero Prop:** Glowing Mushroom with color-changing LED base
- **Costume Notes:** UV-reactive body paint and fabric treatments

**Technical Notes:**
- UV LED fixtures for blacklight wash
- Mirror ball with pin spots for dance sequence
- Color-changing LED puck light in mushroom prop

## **TALE 2: OCTAVIA (The Deep Sea)**

### **Overall Visual Philosophy**
Fluid, weightless environments where lighting becomes the primary set element. Transition from surface brightness to deep sea mystery.

---

### **Scene 1: The Shallows (Pirates & Surface)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Color Palette:** Turquoise (#40E0D0), Sandy Beige, Pure White
- **Lighting:** Water ripple effects using specialized fixtures or mirror reflection
- **Set Piece:** Pirate ship hull cutout suspended above proscenium
- **Props:** Lightweight foam coral formations in bright colors
- **Atmosphere:** Bubble machines for underwater feel

**Technical Notes:**
- Ripple light projector or broken mirror tray for water effects
- Hull: curved wood or painted canvas suspended from grid
- Coral props: carved foam with bright paint finishes

---

### **Scene 2: Shark Territory (Danger Zone)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Color Palette:** Deep Navy (#000080), Steel Grey
- **Lighting:** Side-lighting only (shin busters) for dramatic shadows
- **Effect:** Moving shark silhouette gobos on floor
- **Set:** Jagged rock formations creating narrow passages
- **Atmosphere:** Menacing, claustrophobic environment

**Technical Notes:**
- Shark gobos: custom moving head projections
- Side lights: narrow beams from low angles
- Rock formations: lightweight, modular for flexible staging

---

### **Scene 3 & 4: The Deep Sea & Cave (Bioluminescence)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Color Palette:** Black background with Neon Purple and Green accents
- **Lighting:** Dark stage with tight specials on Octavia
- **Haze:** Water-based fog to catch light beams
- **Set Piece:** Puffy's Lair with silver mylar accents for scale shimmer
- **Props:** Bioluminescent creatures on rods for puppeteering

**Technical Notes:**
- Haze machine: water-based for safety
- Mylar fabric on set pieces for light reflection
- LED strip lighting for creature effects

---

### **Scene 5: The Finale (The Portal)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Hero Prop:** Clear acrylic shard with internal gold lighting
- **Lighting Transition:** Blue water to warm gold wash
- **Effect:** Portal opening with expanding light circles
- **Atmosphere:** Return to surface brightness and clarity

**Technical Notes:**
- Acrylic shard with LED string lights inside
- Color transition: slow fade from blue to amber
- Portal effect: expanding gobo pattern or projection mapping

---

## **TALE 3: ELEMENTAL ODYSSEY (The Islands)**

### **Overall Visual Philosophy**
Organic textures meeting elemental forces. Each scene represents a different natural state - lush life, rocky coast, volcanic destruction, and restoration.

---

### **Scene 1: Kilauea (The Village)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Color Palette:** Lush Green (#228B22), Hibiscus Red, Earthy Brown
- **Lighting:** Warm, cheerful daylight with amber gels
- **Textures:** Thatch materials (raffia, dried grass)
- **Props:** Awa Leaf packaging with banana leaf texture and subtle glow
- **Atmosphere:** Tropical paradise, abundant life

**Technical Notes:**
- Amber gels: Rosco #05, #06 for warm daylight
- Thatch: natural materials for authentic texture
- Subtle LED in prop packaging for magical element

---

### **Scene 2: Island of Riveting Rhythms (Sirens)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Color Palette:** Slate Grey, Ocean Blue, Pearl White
- **Lighting:** Cool, misty blue with lower contrast
- **Set:** Rock formations creating natural amphitheater
- **Hero Prop:** Large gold G-Clef key (foam board construction)
- **Atmosphere:** Coastal, windswept, mystical

**Technical Notes:**
- Blue gels: Rosco #59, #60 for cool ocean feel
- Rock formations: Styrofoam blocks painted and textured
- G-Clef prop: large enough for audience visibility

---

### **Scene 3: Zephyr Island (The Volcano)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Color Palette:** Ash Grey, Magma Red (#FF4500), Charred Black
- **Lighting:** Fire effects with flickering red/orange uplighting
- **Atmosphere:** Heavy fog for volcanic mist
- **Set Piece:** Magma channels with red LED tape behind rock formations
- **Texture:** Scorched earth, volcanic rock, heat distortion

**Technical Notes:**
- LED tape: red/orange color-changing strips
- Fog machine: heavy output for volcanic atmosphere
- Uplighting: fixtures placed low to simulate lava glow

---

### **Scene 4: Finale (Restoration)**
**Visual Language:**
- **Transition:** Fade from red lava to warm daylight wash
- **Effect:** Three sisters with shard creating beam to starfield
- **Color Return:** Gradual reintroduction of lush greens
- **Atmosphere:** Healing, renewal, cosmic connection

**Technical Notes:**
- Color transition: slow crossfade over 30-60 seconds
- Shard effect: tight spotlight with gobo projection
- Starfield: connection back to Solaria's fiber optic backdrop

## **PRODUCTION NOTES & TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS**

### **Universal Elements**
- All scene transitions should be smooth and choreographed
- Lighting cues coordinated with musical score
- Props designed for quick changes and durability
- Safety considerations for all special effects

### **Budget Considerations**
- LED technology for energy efficiency and color control
- Modular set pieces for multiple scene use
- Rental options for specialized lighting equipment
- Volunteer crew training for complex effects

### **Timeline Recommendations**
- 6 weeks for set construction and painting
- 4 weeks for lighting design and programming
- 2 weeks for full technical rehearsals
- 1 week for previews and adjustments
